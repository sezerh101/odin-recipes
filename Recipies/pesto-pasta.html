Pesto pasta

Description

This is an amazing dish that is my personal favourite, it takes the simplistic taste of pasta and adds an extraordinary flavour to it.

It's a very beginner friendly dish and extremely easy to make, making it a fan favourite.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pesto
  • Tuna
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil some water
  2. Put 200g of pasta in once the water has started boiling
  3. Once the pasta has cooked, extract it from the pot into a bowl
  4. Add 15g of pesto into the bowl of pasta and mix until all the pasta is covered
  5. Add a can of tuna, making sure the tuna is broken up into small bits
  6. Add as much parmesan cheese as you like
